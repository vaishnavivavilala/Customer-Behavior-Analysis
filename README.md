# Customer-Behavior-Analysis
Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

Project Overview :
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using SQL, Python, and Power BI to uncover insights about purchasing patterns, customer segmentation, product popularity, and revenue distribution.

The goal of this project is to understand how customers shop, how discounts and subscriptions affect spending, and which products perform best. These insights can help businesses improve marketing strategies, pricing decisions, and customer engagement.

Tools & Technologies Used :
SQL – Data querying and business analysis
Python (Pandas, Jupyter Notebook) – Data exploration and preprocessing
Power BI – Interactive dashboard and visualization
CSV Dataset – Customer shopping data

Key Business Questions Solved

The analysis answers several important business questions such as:
Revenue Analysis
Total revenue generated by male vs female customers
Revenue contribution of different age groups
Customer Behavior
Do subscribed customers spend more than non-subscribers?
Are repeat buyers more likely to subscribe?

Customer segmentation into:
New customers
Returning customers
Loyal customers
Product Insights
Top 5 products with the highest average review ratings
Top 3 most purchased products within each category
Marketing Insights
Customers who used discounts but still spent above average
Products with the highest discount purchase rate

Logistics Analysis
Comparison of average purchase amount between Standard and Express shipping
Customer Segmentation Logic
Customers are segmented based on previous purchases:
New Customers → 1 purchase
Returning Customers → 2–10 purchases
Loyal Customers → More than 10 purchases
This helps businesses understand customer loyalty and retention.

Key Insights:
Certain products receive higher ratings and purchase frequency
Discounts influence purchasing behavior
Loyal customers contribute significantly to revenue
Subscription programs may increase customer spending and retention
